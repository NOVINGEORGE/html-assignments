    cpu

    A central processing unit (cpu),also refered to as a central processor unit,is the hardware within a computer that carries out the instruction of a computer program by performing the basic arithmatical,logical,and input/output operation of the system.the term has been in use in the computer industery at least since the early 1960s.the form,design,and implementation of cpus have changed over the course of thier history,but their fundamental operation remains much the same

    alu
